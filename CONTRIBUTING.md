# CONTRIBUTOR LICENSE AGREEMENT & PROFIT SHARING

By submitting code (Pull Requests) to the QCK repository, you agree to the following terms:

1. **Copyright Assignment:** You grant Benedict M. Wyneken the irrevocable right to include your code in the QCK framework and license it commercially.
2. **Profit Sharing (The Bounty):** We believe in rewarding crucial open-source contributions. If your specific, accepted code significantly drives commercial licensing deals, you are eligible for a revenue share:
   - If the commercial gross revenue of the QCK project exceeds €500,000, active core contributors share a pool of 1% of the revenue.
   - If revenue exceeds €2,000,000, this pool increases up to 5%.
   - *Note:* The qualification of a "core contributor" and the exact proportional distribution of the revenue share lie at the sole discretion of the project founder, based on the actual impact of the merged commits.
3. **Credits:** All merged contributors will be permanently listed in the repository's credits.
